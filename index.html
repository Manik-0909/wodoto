Data Scout in Football

Remote / Club HQ
Data & Analytics
Role Overview

As a Data Scout, you'll play a critical role in identifying football talent through data-driven insights. You’ll help us spot undervalued players, rising stars, and strategic signings by analyzing performance metrics, scouting reports, and contextual statistics across global leagues.

Key Responsibilities
Monitor global leagues and tournaments for high-potential players using data platforms.
Create detailed player shortlists tailored to club recruitment needs.
Cross-reference statistical findings with video and traditional scouting reports.
Collaborate with analysts, coaches, and recruitment staff to align data scouting with football strategy.
Maintain and update databases on target players, trends, and market opportunities.
Requirements
  • Strong understanding of football performance data (e.g., Wyscout, StatsBomb, Opta, SkillCorner).
  • Proficiency in Excel, Python, or R for data analysis.
  • Ability to interpret and communicate findings clearly to non-technical stakeholders.
  • Passion for football and awareness of global football markets.
  • Previous experience in scouting, analytics, or a football research role is a plus.
